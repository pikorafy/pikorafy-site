NordVPN and Surfshark are two of the most popular VPN services on the market, and they are now both owned by the same parent company, Nord Security. Despite the shared ownership, they remain separate products with distinct pricing, features, and user experiences. Here is how they compare in 2026.

## Overview

**NordVPN** is a premium VPN provider known for its massive server network, advanced security features, and strong reputation built over years of independent audits. It is based in Panama and offers specialty servers, built-in threat protection, and the Meshnet feature for private device networking.

**Surfshark** positions itself as a budget-friendly VPN that punches above its weight. Based in the Netherlands, it offers unlimited simultaneous connections, a clean interface, and a growing feature set that includes CleanWeb ad blocking, MultiHop connections, and a bundled antivirus option.

## Key differences

### Speed

Both VPNs use WireGuard-based protocols for top performance. NordVPN's NordLynx protocol consistently delivers some of the fastest speeds in the industry. Surfshark's WireGuard implementation is also fast, though benchmark tests typically show NordVPN with a slight edge on long-distance connections. For everyday browsing, streaming, and downloads, both are more than fast enough.

### Server network

NordVPN operates over 6,400 servers in 111 countries. Surfshark runs over 3,200 servers in 100 countries. NordVPN's larger network means less congestion during peak times and more options for finding a fast nearby server.

### Security and privacy

Both providers offer AES-256 encryption, kill switches, and no-logs policies verified by independent audits. NordVPN goes further with Double VPN, Onion over VPN, and a full threat protection suite that blocks malware, ads, and trackers at the system level. Surfshark offers CleanWeb for ad and tracker blocking and MultiHop for routing through two servers, but its feature set is more streamlined.

### Simultaneous connections

This is Surfshark's biggest advantage. It allows unlimited simultaneous connections on a single account, meaning you can protect every device in your household without worrying about limits. NordVPN caps connections at 10, which is generous but still a limit.

### Streaming

Both VPNs reliably unblock Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, and other major streaming platforms. NordVPN's SmartPlay technology is purpose-built for streaming access. Surfshark performs well but can occasionally require switching servers to find one that works with a specific platform.

### Apps and usability

NordVPN's apps are feature-rich with an interactive server map and detailed settings. Surfshark's apps are clean, modern, and slightly simpler to navigate. Both support all major platforms including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and browser extensions.

## Pros and cons

### NordVPN

**Pros:**
- Fastest speeds in benchmark tests
- Larger server network across more countries
- Advanced features like Double VPN, Meshnet, and Onion over VPN
- Full threat protection suite with malware blocking
- Proven track record with independent security audits

**Cons:**
- More expensive than Surfshark on every plan tier
- Limited to 10 simultaneous connections
- App interface can feel cluttered with so many features
- Short-term monthly pricing is steep

### Surfshark

**Pros:**
- Unlimited simultaneous connections
- Significantly cheaper, especially on long-term plans
- Clean, intuitive app design
- MultiHop and CleanWeb included on all plans
- Bundled antivirus option with Surfshark One

**Cons:**
- Smaller server network
- Slightly slower on long-distance connections
- Fewer specialty server options
- Streaming access can be inconsistent on some platforms
- Netherlands jurisdiction is within EU data laws

## When to use each

**Choose NordVPN if:**
- Speed is your top priority and you want the fastest connections
- You need specialty servers like Double VPN or Onion over VPN
- Built-in malware and ad blocking at the system level matters to you
- You want the largest possible server network
- You prefer a VPN with the longest independent audit history

**Choose Surfshark if:**
- You want to protect unlimited devices on one account
- Budget is a primary concern and you want the lowest price
- You need a simple, clean VPN app without complexity
- You are covering a large household with many devices
- You want antivirus bundled with your VPN subscription

## Verdict

**Surfshark** is the better value pick in 2026. Its unlimited connections and lower pricing make it ideal for families and budget-conscious users who still want strong security. **NordVPN** is the better overall VPN with faster speeds, more features, and a larger server network, but you pay more for those advantages.

If you can afford NordVPN's long-term pricing, it is the stronger product. If you want solid VPN protection at the lowest cost with no device limits, Surfshark is hard to beat.
